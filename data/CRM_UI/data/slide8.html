    Sales Page: Create leads manually (Sales Mngr)
    • User-story:
      When: Sales Manager clicks on the button “Create the lead manually”
      Then: pop up screen will display:
      In the left top - Client’s data (type,date, quantity of guests, event place)
      In the right top - Client ( client’s type ( radio-button visible as checkbox private/ business, Client's name, Client’s phone, Client’s wife name, Client’s wife phone , Email, City, Address)
      Under client’s data -> dropdown with sales representatives whom the sales manager can assign to this client.
      Event types are hard-coded as options to the drop-down list.
