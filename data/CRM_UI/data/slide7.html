    • Sales_Representative Dashboard
      • User-story: Sales_Representative Dashboard
        When: User logs in under Sales_Representative credentials
        Then: Sales_Representative should be able to view:
        - Own leads by numbers with status
        - Tasks for the current day

      • Tasks for the current day per given Sales Representative Statuses are as follows:
        בוטל עקב אי שליחה -
        Canceled due to non-sending SMS
        מסורב -
        Refused
        בוטל ע״י בעל האירוע -
        Canceled by the event owner
        נסגר שירותים ללא איזי -
        Services closed without Easy2Give
        פגישה התקיימה ונחתמה -
        A meeting was held and signed
        ללא מענה -
        Unanswered
        בתהליך -
        in process
        נוצר קשר ראשוני -
        Initial contact was made
        טרם טופל -
        Not yet taken care of
      • Scenario: Leads go to Field Agent for processing:
        When : Lead is successfully Sold
        Then: The Lead is forwarded to a Field Agent
        And : Such Leads become available for Field Agent for processing
        And: The Sold Leads are no longer visbile for Sales Representatives, i.e. under the general Leads view.
